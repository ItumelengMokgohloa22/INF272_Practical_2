Biography and Background

My name is Bohlale Thosago, and I am a 22-year-old student from Polokwane. I have always been passionate about technology, knowledge, and the way information is organized, which is why I chose to study Information Science. This field excites me because it combines technology with data management, offering endless opportunities to improve how people access and use information. Growing up in Polokwane, I was always curious about how systems work, from libraries to digital platforms. This curiosity led me to explore various aspects of information management, including databases, digital archiving, and cybersecurity. As the world becomes more digital, I believe that organizing and securing information is more important than ever..

Hobbies

In my free time, I enjoy engaging in activities that challenge my mind, spark creativity, and help me relax. One of my biggest passions is reading, as it allows me to explore new ideas, gain knowledge, and immerse myself in different perspectives. Whether it’s books on technology, self-improvement, or fiction, reading helps me expand my understanding of the world. I also have a deep interest in technology and coding. I love experimenting with new software, learning about programming, and exploring the latest advancements in artificial intelligence. I find joy in problem-solving and creating efficient digital solutions, which align well with my studies in Information Science.
